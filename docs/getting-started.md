## Getting Started

1. Open the [editor](/edit) and create a slideshow.
2. Add slides and content in the editor.
3. Open **Settings** and create at least one display.
4. In the slideshow sidebar, click **Mount** and pick a display.
5. Open the display URL shown in Settings (`/display/<name>`).

## Notes

- Display names are case-sensitive.
- If a display is mounted, you can unmount per slideshow or with **Unmount All**.
- You can [view a list](/show) of all available display and direct slideshow endpoints.
